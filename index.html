ABOUT ME


I am an associate professor (tenure-track) at the College of Intelligence and Computing of Tianjin University, working with Prof. Qinghua Hu. I received the Ph.D. degree from Xidian University under the supervision of Prof. Xinbo Gao in 2020. From April. 2019 to July. 2020, I am a research assistant at IDEA Lab in University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, supervised by Prof. Dinggang Shen. My research interests are on computer vision and deep learning, particularly multimodal learning and image synthesis.
